About the developer

I'm Winnie Wandia, the visionary developer behind Road Trip Companion. Raised in Nairobi, Kenya, my journey into app development stems from a profound love for exploration and technology. I merged technical skills with an adventurous spirit, exploring Kenya's hidden gems during college. Road Trip Companion was born from my desire to elevate fellow explorers' road trip experiences. My coding journey reflects tireless dedication and an unwavering pursuit of perfection, mastering various APIs and libraries to create a user-friendly, culturally sensitive app. Each line of code captures Kenya's beauty, ensuring features resonate with the spirit of adventure. As the developer, I invite users to join a digital journey mirroring the excitement, wonder, and safety of a well-planned road trip in Kenya.
